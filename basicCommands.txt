Type any of the following items in "" to do the corresponding command.

	"N" or "S" or "E" or "W" 
		Entering one of these will move your player in that cardinal direction 
	
	"look"
		When you enter a room, the description of the room will be displayed
		followed by the items in the room that you can put in your inventory,
		and the directions that you can move from that room. Use this command
		to have the state of the room said again.
	
	"take <item in room>"
		Use this command to pick up an item from the room you are in. The item
		will be taken from the room and added to your inventory.
	
	"items"
		Use this command to see the items you have in your inventory.
		
	"drop <item in your inventory>"
		Use this command to remove an item from your inventory and drop it in
		the room you are currently in.
			
	"save <what you want to save your game as>"	
		Will save the current state of your game, that way you can pick up 
		where you left off.				

	"quit"
		Use this command to safely exit from the game. The game does NOT save
		before it quits. All progress will be lost unless you save before you
		quit!
		
Depending on the adventure, there can be special commands that can be used. The 
adventure creator will notify you when and what those commands are. To learn more
about how to create your own text adventure please reference the README.txt.
